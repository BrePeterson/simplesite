A Few Things About Me

I am a business marketing student at the University of Northern Colorado. After college I am interested in a career in Digital Marketing and eventually want to own a small business. Apart from my education I enjoy exploring Colorado, attending concerts, pursuing photography, and finding calm in the busyness of life.

Colorado Native

I am a born and raised Coloradan! My younger brother and I grew up playing competitive sports which allowed us to travel all over the state to compete. It is here that my love for traveling and exploring was born. Though I have spent all of my 22 years in Colorado, I have never been skiing or snowboarding, but instead love to escape to the mountians for a weekend to relax. Colorado is glorious in so many ways, and has so much to offer from city living to open spaces and the Rocky Mountains!

Career Interests

After graduating from UNCO I plan on pursuing a career in Digital Marketing. I am interested in the more creative aspect of marketing as well as event marketing and sports or enterntainment marketing. I also want to own a small business that is ran out of a trailer, so that I am able to continue to travel and explore the world!

More About Me

I enjoy spending lots of time with my family! We have two dogs who take up a lot of our love and attention. I also enjoy traveling to watch my younger brother play college baseball. I have a growing love for all things vintage, western and anything to do with photography!
